🔧 Fleet Maintenance & Technical Support

Corsair Airlines is committed to ensuring the highest standards of safety and reliability for our passengers. Our dedicated Technical Maintenance Department oversees all aspects of fleet maintenance, ensuring that each aircraft meets rigorous safety and operational standards.

🛠️ Maintenance Responsibilities

  • Ensuring continuing airworthiness of all aircraft
  • Managing logistics and maintenance operations
  • Conducting quality assurance to uphold safety standards

📍 Maintenance Locations

  • Primary base at Paris Orly Airport
  • Additional facilities in the Indian Ocean region

🔒 Certifications

Our Technical Management holds EASA/DGAC Part M and Part 145 certifications, guaranteeing that our aircraft are operated under maximum safety conditions.
